Downside Up

Enders Pearl uses the "Ender Pearl" mechanic from Minecraft to study the possibilities of designing many varied levels with only one simple mechanic.

Development process

I made this game in my spare time over ~2 months. The point of the project was to study level design of platformers while sticking to just one very simple mechanic. Most of the development time went into designing levels on paper and playtesting them in-game until they felt good. I managed to design 15 distinct levels that teaches the player the different uses of the main mechanic while slowly ramping up the difficulty.

Game and Rules

The game is a standard side-scrolling platformer, but uses the "Ender Pearl" mechanic from Minecraft. You can throw pearls that teleport you to their location if they collide with terrain. The game is split into 15 levels that teach you the various uses of the pearls.
