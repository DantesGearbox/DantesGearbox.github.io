Pizza Panic!

A local multiplayer racing game, similar to Crazy Taxi, but instead of delivering people, you're delivering pizza.

Development Process

This game was made over three weekends by me and a team of university friends using Unity. Design-wise I worked on defining the main game idea, while programming-wise I worked on handling inputs from up to four controllers and also made the UI and menus.

Game and Rules

Pizza panic is a local multiplayer racing game where you have to be the person to deliver the most pizza. Your fuel level goes down all the time, but whenever you pick up a pizza, you magically get more. First person to run out of gas looses! Up to four players can drive around and even steal each others pizzas, by driving into each other and knocking the pizzas off of each other.
