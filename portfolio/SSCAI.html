Starcraft Broodwar AI

A Starcraft bot written in C++. It is participating in the Student Starcraft AI Tournament (SSCAIT) and has a 31.8% winrate after 2127 games.

Project

The AI is made using the open-source c++ framework BWAPI. We have made a Protoss bot that works on any map. Besides this it can plan out a build order, place buildings to conserve space, scout out the enemies strategy and launch effective attacks using micro management.

Process

The bot was developed by four programmers over 3 months as a university project. My main responsibility in the project was to create the architecture to support our bot. Besides this I was responsible for creating effective building placement and resource collection.

Results

The winrate of the bot can be seen in image 3. The team wasn't entirely happy with this win-rate concidering the relative complexity of our AI. However, other participants in SSCAIT have created highly elaborate AIs that are hard to compete with.

Our bot, called DAIDOES, is still live and participating in SSCAIT. See it here.
