Downside Up

Downside Up is a psychedelic platformer where you turn your downwards momentum upside down to reach new heights and complete levels.

Development process

I made this game for Kaktus Jam 2018 in Copenhagen. My goal was to design the simplest possible main mechanic and then game-feeling the shit out of everything.



Having more levels would be nice, but instead I spend all the time on testing out fancy effects. The visual effects can be seen in the gifs. Beyond the visual effects I give the player a couple frames after landing where their second jump is still available, just to make sure the game feels responsive and fair.

Game and Rules

To progress through levels you must build up momentum by falling and then turn that momentum around to jump up with the same speed you were just falling with. The concept is simple once you understand it, but might take a little experimenting as it is quite different from normal double jumps.
