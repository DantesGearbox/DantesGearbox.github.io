Parasite

Parasite is a walking simulator where you explore a destroyed city, while conversing with an alien intent on killing the human race.

Development Process

This game was made for a university course and was supposed to focus on world building. Therefore I was heavily involved in creating the story and the world of this game. I was also the programmer on this game, which means that I was responsible for setting up the scene and making the cutscenes all fit to the designers intent.
The team consisted of 2 programmers and 2 designers and the development took place over 3 months.

About the game

The game takes place in a post-apocalyptic Texas. The protagonaist is being controlled by the antagonist, an alien parasite that wants to kill the last remaining humans on earth. Throughout the game the antagonist tries to convince the protagonist to lead the parasite to the hideout of the humans. Whether to do this or not is up to the player.
