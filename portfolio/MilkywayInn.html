Milkyway Inn

Milkyway Inn is a tycoon/colonist game where the player builds and manages a space station inside an asteroid.

Development Process

Milkyway Inn was made for a university course where we were given most of a semester to make a game like a profesional studio would. The whole development process has taken place over approximately 3Â½ months, resulting in a very polished, ambitious game.

The Teamwork

My role on the team was a mix of programmer and designer. As such, a lot of my work consisted of communicating between designers and programmers and implementing our design into the code base. Examples are implementing UI elements and the behavior of NPCs through a state machine.

Milkyway Inn was made by a team of student developers based in Copenhagen, Denmark. The team consisted of 4 programmers and 2 designers. Besides this, two freelancers have made assets for the project.

The game

The appeal of Milkyway Inn is in construction play and creative problem solving. Visitors from around the galaxy will come and visit your station and it is your job as station manager to fulfill your visitors needs while maintaining the stations utilities.
