Who I am Game programmer and designer

Hello there!

My name is Mads Engberg, and I am a developer based in Copenhagen.

I've been developing games and organizing game jams for over two years now and I'm thoroughly in love with doing both.

I am both a programmer and a designer, but I especially adore the part of game development that lies on the intersection between those two; the place where technology and design work together to create gameplay, game feel and user experience. As such I am great at gameplay programming and at facilitating communication between game designers and programmers.

I'm a big fan of creating game mechanics that you want to master.
