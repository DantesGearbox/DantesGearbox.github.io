About me

My name is Mads Engberg and I am a gameplay programmer based in Copenhagen.

I have been developing games for 8 years and commercially for 5 years. I have a MSc in Games and a BSc in Computer Science along with strong skills in Unity and C#, Unreal Engine and C++ and AI and algorithms.

However, what really sets me apart is my keen eye for design. I am a programmer first and a designer second, but I especially love working at the intersection of those two; the place where expressive gameplay and juicy game feel lives.

As such, I excel at communicating with designers and programmers alike and being mindful of everyone's perspectives.

Besides game-making, I have also organised and participated in many large game jams. I have been lead organiser for Level One Jam, regional organiser for Global Game Jam and a board member and organiser for Nordic Game Jam. I also have experience as a teacher in making games with Unity. This gives me excellent skills in organisation, teaching and public speaking.
