About me

My name is Mads Engberg and I am a game developer based in Copenhagen.

I have been developing games for 4 years and commercially for 2 years. I have a BSc in Software Development and strong skills in Unity, graphics programming, AI and algorithms. However what sets me apart is my keen eye for design. I am a programmer first and a designer second, but I am especially experienced in the intersection between those two; the place where gameplay, game feel and user-experience lives.

 As such I also excel at communicating with designers and being mindful of their perspective.

Besides game-making, I have also organised and participated in many large game jams. I have been lead organiser for Level One Jam, been regional organiser for Global Game Jam and I am a board member and organiser for Nordic Game Jam. I also have experience being a workshop-runner and teacher in game-making.
